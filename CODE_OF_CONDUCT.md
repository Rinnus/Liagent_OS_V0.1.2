# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We aim for a respectful, inclusive, and technically rigorous community.

Examples of expected behavior:
- Constructive technical feedback
- Respectful disagreement focused on ideas
- Clear communication and reproducible reports

Unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Disruptive behavior that blocks collaboration

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and
PRs that violate this code of conduct.

Serious or repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from
project spaces.
